Avatar: The Legend of Aang

The plot tells the story of a boy from a tribe of air nomads, Aang, who is able to conquer the element of air. At the age of 11, he learned that he was the Avatar, the master of the four elements. The world is mired in a long-term war between the Fire Nation and the rest of the world, in which certain people are able to conquer one of the four elements - water, earth, fire or air. Aang is the only one capable of conquering all the elements and as the Avatar, he must save the world from destruction by the Fire Nation. With his new friends, Katara and Sokka, Aang sets out to master the elements, along the way they are pursued by the exiled prince of the Fire Nation, Zuko, who wants to regain his honor by capturing the Avatar.
